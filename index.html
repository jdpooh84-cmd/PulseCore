What is PulseCore? ↓
PulseCore is an AI-powered marketing tool built for local business owners like you.

It scouts real local news and events in your city, writes a professional video script in your voice, and gives you ready-to-post captions for every social platform.

You'll need a free Anthropic API key to get started. Takes about 2 minutes to set up.
